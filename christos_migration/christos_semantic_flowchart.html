Semantic Migration of χριστός

From pharmaceutical adjective to opaque proper name  |  c. 460 BCE – 155 CE

Sources: LSJ s.v. χριστός (Liddell–Scott–Jones 9th ed.); Rahlfs–Hanhart LXX; Nestle–Aland 28th ed.; Niese Josephus; Psalms of Solomon (Wright, OTP II); Ignatius of Antioch (Schoedel 1985); Justin Martyr (Marcovich 1994). ★ = Psalms of Solomon is not LXX canon. Analysis follows the calque model (χριστός = semantic calque; Μεσσίας = transliteration loanword).
